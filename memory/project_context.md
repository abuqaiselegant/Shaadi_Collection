Shaadi Collection is a digital wedding decoration catalog built for the Indian market.

**Why:** Replaces a physical lookbook — customers browse items and inquire via WhatsApp. Prices are in ₹ (Indian Rupees).

**How to apply:** Frame all suggestions (copy, currency, features) in the context of Indian weddings and Indian customers.
